    Primary

    Brand Color

    Sour Apple is the signature brand color of Educators for Excellence. The apple is a familiar gift you give a teacher, and the sour color becomes a metaphor of the challenges Educators for Excellence bites into. It is important that color-use always appears fresh; even edible. As a secondary color, green is humble. But with it’s saturation, sour apple commands a brave presence.

    Secondary

    Analogous Palette

    The analogous palette is designed to be used for less contrast. This palette creates mood. The mood of Educators for Excellence is inspired by optimistic. It is evident in their growth mindset and solutions orientation.

    Secondary

    Complementary Palette

    The complementary palette is designed to be used for contrast. This palette creates impact. The bravery of Educators for Excellence is demonstrated in the contrasts of color and value to these hues. These colors are used for content that needs to pop out at the viewer.

    Secondary

    Neutral Palette

    The neutral palette is designed to be used for background or low impact application. It is the responsiblity of the neutral palette to support the brand color as the primary color impression.
