    Declaration Sign-up Board

    If you would like to create a poster reproduction of the Declaration sign-up board, use the specifications provided here. You will find the artwork labeled “-Press” that should be sent to the printer. The Declaration should always carry the national brand. Do not replace the logo with your chapter lock-up. It is important to demonstrate we all share the same principles and beliefs.

    Flat Size 24” x 36” with .125” bleeds
    Finished size 24” x 36”
    Full color - one side
    Mounted on 3/16” foamcore

    Grab some sharpies and sign in the ample margins or on the Declaration itself. This is a placard for a wall of signatures.

    For Union Printing: Contact Richard Mason, Harris Litho, rich@harrisplus.com
